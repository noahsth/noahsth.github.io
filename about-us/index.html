Our Story

Chhaya Photography was founded with a simple vision: to capture life's most precious moments with artistry and authenticity. What started as a passion has grown into a full-service photography studio serving clients across Texas.

With years of experience and a deep understanding of light, composition, and emotion, we specialize in creating timeless images that tell your unique story. From intimate weddings to grand celebrations, from personal portraits to corporate events, we bring the same level of dedication and creativity to every shoot.

Our approach combines technical expertise with genuine connection. We believe that the best photographs come from understanding our clients, their vision, and the moments that matter most to them.
